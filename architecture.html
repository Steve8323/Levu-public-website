Design Philosophy

Why Cross-Domain?

A digital-only processor wastes the natural strengths of light. Analog operations like matrix multiply are trivially efficient in optics. Quantum operations need optical feed-forward. Levu's XPU handles all three ‚Äî switching between domains at the speed of light via an all-optical ADC bridge.

Digital Domain
Optical Logic Gates
Boolean logic, branching, and control flow ‚Äî fully optical, no electron switching delays.
Analog Domain
Math Operations
Matrix multiplication, convolution, and activation functions at the speed of light.
Quantum Domain
CV Feed-Forward
All-optical feed-forward for continuous variable quantum computation ‚Äî no measurement collapse.
Domain Bridge
All-Optical ADC
Converts analog optical signals to digital optical signals without ever leaving the photonic domain.
