Manufacturing

Foundry Strategy

Levu designs are tapeout-ready for major silicon photonics foundries including imec, GlobalFoundries, and LIGENTEC. We use standard PDK-compliant components wherever possible, reserving proprietary nonlinear layers for our differentiated building blocks.

Multi-project wafer (MPW) shuttles reduce per-chip NRE costs during development. Full-wafer production runs begin once design freeze is achieved.

Our co-design methodology tightly couples fabrication tolerances back into the photonic simulation loop ‚Äî ensuring what we simulate is what we build.
